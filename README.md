## 🟣 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer purchase behavior, regional revenue performance, and product profitability using SQL, Excel, and Power BI. The goal is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights for effective MIS reporting and decision-making.

---

## 🟣 Problem Statement

The organization lacked visibility into customer retention, revenue contribution, and regional performance due to fragmented transactional data. This made it difficult for management to monitor sales growth and identify high-value customers.

---

## 🟣 Project Objective

- Analyze customer purchase patterns  
- Identify repeat customers  
- Track monthly revenue trends  
- Measure product category profitability  
- Improve MIS reporting efficiency  

---

## 🟣 Tools & Technologies Used

- MySQL  
- Microsoft Excel  
- Power BI  
- Power Query  
- DAX  

---

## 🟣 Dataset Description

The dataset consists of:

- Customer Data  
- Order Transactions  
- Product Cost Information  

---

## 🟣 Key KPIs Developed

- Total Revenue  
- Total Profit  
- Profit Margin (%)  
- Average Order Value  
- Monthly Revenue Growth (%)  
- Repeat Customer %  
- Region-wise Revenue  
- Category Contribution  

---

## 🟣 Dashboard Insights

- Identified revenue contribution from repeat customers  
- Evaluated regional sales performance  
- Highlighted low-profit product categories  
- Centralized KPI reporting for improved business monitoring
